STP Solar offers a referral program to reward customers who refer friends, colleagues, and family members for solar installations. The referral program is subject to the following terms:

Referral Fee Structure

Referral fees are paid based on the total value of the solar system installation:

  • R30,000 - R80,000: R500 referral fee
  • R80,001 - R150,000: R1,000 referral fee
  • R150,001 - R250,000: R1,500 referral fee
  • R250,001 - R500,000: R2,500 referral fee
  • Above R500,000: R5,000 referral fee

Program Terms & Conditions

  • Eligibility: Referral fees are only paid for successful installations where the referred customer mentions the referrer's name during the initial consultation.
  • Payment Timeline: Referral fees are processed within 30 days of the solar system installation completion and final inspection.
  • One Referral Per Customer: Each referred customer can only be claimed by one referrer. In case of disputes, the first person to submit the referral will be credited.
  • Installation Completion Required: Referral fees are only paid after the solar system is fully installed, commissioned, and the final payment has been received from the customer.
  • System Value: The referral fee is calculated based on the total contract value of the solar system installation, including all components, installation, and services.
  • Payment Method: Referral fees will be paid via electronic transfer to the referrer's nominated bank account.
  • Tax Implications: Referral fees may be subject to income tax. Referrers are responsible for declaring these amounts to SARS if required.
  • Program Changes: STP Solar reserves the right to modify or discontinue the referral program at any time with 30 days' notice.
  • Exclusions: Referral fees are not applicable for:
    • Existing customers upgrading their systems
    • Commercial installations over R1,000,000
    • Installations where the referrer is a STP Solar employee or contractor
    • Installations that are cancelled or not completed

How to Participate

  • Submit your referral through our official referral form on the website
  • Ensure the referred person mentions your name during their initial consultation
  • Referral fees will be automatically processed upon successful installation completion
